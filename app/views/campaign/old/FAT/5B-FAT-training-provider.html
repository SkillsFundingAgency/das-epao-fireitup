About the apprenticeship:

This apprenticeship offers an excellent way to introduce new talent to your business. The programme is very flexible so it can be tailored to a variety of industries/occupations. The programme offers a great grounding and covers all the core elements of working in a modern office.

We deliver the programme using a blend of 1:1 teaching in the workplace, workshops and e-learning. Training is designed with you the employer to ensure it meets your needs. Staff work closely with you throughout the programme to ensure that you are getting the best possible value from the scheme and tangible company benefits.

We do not yet have published achievement or satisfaction scores but we do have externally validated data available that we are happy to share

Provider information

Boom Training is based in Hampshire but covers Surrey, Thames Valley, London and parts of the midlands.

We pride ourselves on:

  • A high level of support to employers and apprentices through fortnightly visits to the workplace
  • A solutions-based approach through listening and offering impartial advice on how you can make the most of the apprenticeship reforms, including the Levy

Our portfolio includes Management, Business Admin, Customer Service, Sales, Recruitment, Childcare, Supporting Teaching in schools and Hospitality (including professional chef training). However it is constantly evolving and we are developing a number of vocational specialisms so if you don't see what you are looking for, please do call and ask.
