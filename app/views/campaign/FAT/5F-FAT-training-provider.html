About the apprenticeship:

Our apprenticeships are work-based.

We complete a Training Needs Analysis and use BKSB as our initial diagnostic assessment tool. Using Skills Forward, we create an Individual Learning Plan and map how the 20% off the job will be achieved.

Each apprentice is assigned a trainer who supports them throughout their apprenticeship, delivering a 2 Â½ hour training session each month (2 consecutive face to face visits and then a remote visit).

After training sessions, the trainer gives a detailed progress review which is uploaded to OneFile, allowing all parties to check apprentice progress.

We include a mock end-point assessment prior to gateway review with the apprentice's appointed line manager, to ensure end-point assessment readiness.

Provider information

Training Event Safety Solutions (known as The TESS Group) is a skills focused, training and development provider.

We have been delivering apprenticeships for the past 7 years, so you can rest assured we have the experience to ensure you and your apprentices get the best from the training. We provide active collaboration, bespoke course tailoring and real return on investment. Our apprenticeships are work-based and we deliver nationally, so we can deliver across your multiple sites.

We are Ofsted grade 2 rated and hold the Matrix accreditation and Investors in People gold standard. We have won numerous awards for our apprenticeships; most recently we have been named as:

  • Gold winner: Apprenticeship Employer category (2016 Apprenticeships 4 England awards)
  • Silver winner: Training Provider category (2016 Apprenticeships 4 England awards)
