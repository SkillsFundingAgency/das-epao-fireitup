About the apprenticeship:

This apprenticeship is about playing an important support role within a business or organisation. We design and deliver bespoke training, built around the needs of apprentices and employers, including one-to-one support from an expert assessor, on-site training, an online learning platform, remote support, workshops and more. We can support you through every step of the process, from recruitment to accessing funding. Whether you are looking to recruit and train new apprentices or develop the skills of existing staff we can help.

Provider information

We provide apprenticeships to meet the needs of your organisation, including in house training, 1:1 support, workshops, online learning & more. Primarily operating in SE England & London, we have capacity to deliver training across England. We create unique packages of support built around the needs of staff & aligned to your workforce vision. Our training is suitable for new & existing staff ranging from L2-5, supporting career progression & addressing skills gaps. Our free recruitment support & flexible sign up dates mean we can work at your pace. We care passionately about our clients & our apprentices, offering the services required to build a highly skills, flexible & motivated workforce for the future.
