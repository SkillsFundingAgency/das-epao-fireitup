About the apprenticeship:

Effective administration is a crucial element of business success; ensuring everything is running smoothly behind the scenes, is essential in delivering end value to the customer.

The Business Administrator Standard develops the knowledge, skills and behaviour of those in an administration role, helping them to develop their key competences to perform their role efficiently and with integrity. They will be expected to demonstrate strong communication skills, adopting a proactive approach to their role. Showing initiative, managing priorities and time will also be assessed and support provided where necessary. Other core elements of this Apprenticeship include problem solving skills, decision making and the potential for people management.

Provider information

Intec is one of the UK's leading nationwide work-based training providers specialising in the delivery apprenticeships from Intermediate to Higher Levels.

For over 30 years we have been working in partnership with local, national and international companies to:

  • Recruit apprentices for their workforce
  • Enhance the skills and qualifications of their existing employees
  • Improve their organisations business performance

You will benefit from key account management ensuring you are fully supported in the development of your apprenticeship and staff training programmes. We provide high quality training and assessment in the work place which is accredited through awarding organisations such as City and Guilds and The Charted Management Institute (CMI).
