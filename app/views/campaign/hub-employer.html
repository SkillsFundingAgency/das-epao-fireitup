EXPLORE YOUR OPTIONS

Still deciding whether apprenticeships are right for your business? Take a look around – we’ve gathered plenty of valuable hints and tips to help you make your decision.

Where should I start?

SHAPE YOUR APPRENTICESHIP

When you’re ready to begin your search, use our online tool to find the right apprenticeship for your business and team up with your favourite training provider.

SPREAD THE WORD

Next, you’ll need to create a vacancy. You can work with your training provider to do this, or you could complete it yourself – the choice is yours. Once your vacancy has been approved, it will be advertised here for free. On average, this service is accessed over 260,000 times each week, so it’s the ideal place to promote your apprenticeship.

CHOOSE YOUR APPRENTICE

Now for the exciting bit – choosing your apprentice. You can manage the applications using our online tool or via your own website, and you could join forces with your training provider to work out who’s the right fit.

FIRE IT UP

Congratulations – you’ve hired an apprentice! The successful applicant will be added to your account, and you’ll soon experience the energy, talent and enthusiasm an apprentice brings.

I’d like to find out more – where should I start?

Whether you’re just beginning your apprenticeship journey or you have a few last-minute questions, you’ll find all the key details below.

Funding your apprenticeship

You can get help from the government to pay for apprenticeship training and assessment. The amount you get depends on whether you pay the apprenticeship levy or not.

Find out more

Finding the right apprenticeship

Once you’ve decided to get started, you’ll need to seek out the right apprenticeship to suit your business needs. We’ve got lots of information and a search tool to help you find the right match.

Find out more

Choosing a training provider

Apprentices spend at least 20% of their time on off-the-job training, so you’ll need to choose a training provider to carry this out. Our online tool will help you search by location, job role or keyword.

Find out more

Hiring an apprentice

When the apprenticeship and training provider are in place, it’s time to find the right person to become your new apprentice. Read our guide to making that all-important decision.

Find out more

If you don’t pay the apprenticeship levy - for the time being, you will manage your apprentices directly with your chosen training provider.
