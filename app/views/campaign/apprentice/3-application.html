So, you've found your ideal apprenticeship?

If you haven't, there are many places employers advertise for apprentices. For starters, try searching the Find an apprenticeship website.

Apply for the job and send the application to the employer

Get help how to apply for your chosen apprenticeship - including creating a good CV and writing your cover letter.

Wait for the applications to be shortlisted

This can be the nail-biting bit, where you're waiting to hear back from the employer. Sometimes it might help to check when the closing date was for applications - this should give you a clue as to when they'll be in touch.

If you’re on the shortlist, you’ll be invited for an interview with the employer

This is when you need to brush up your research into the organisation for your forthcoming interview.
