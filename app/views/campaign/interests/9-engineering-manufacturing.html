The apprenticeship I'm currently on was one of the best opportunities I had come across. I had no clue what I wanted to do for a job.

I knew further education wasn't for me, but I couldn't think of anything else I would love. As soon as I saw the advert for my apprenticeship, I just knew it was perfect.

I'd be working with chocolate and learning the skills created hundreds of years ago with one of the biggest food and drink companies in the world. When I saw that, alongside the apprenticeship, I would also be working to get a degree, I was over the moon! It seemed to be the best of both worlds while also earning a wage.

Employer real stories

Apprentices are our workforce of the future

How do you find the right training for your apprentices?

We are an employer provider.

How do you collaborate with your training provider?

We are an employer provider and deliver the vast majority of our apprenticeship programmes in-house. For our specialised apprenticeships we collaborate with training providers for regular meetings, reviewing the progress and development needs of the apprentices.

How do you find the right apprentices?

We have our own resourcing team and run campaigns.

What help did you have with the process?

Our internal training, human resources and recruitment team work closely together to ensure consistently enhancing our apprentice recruitment processes by analysing feedback from managers and apprentices along with the company's statistical data.
