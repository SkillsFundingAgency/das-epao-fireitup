About the apprenticeship:

This apprenticeship is ideal for those who need to develop their skills, knowledge and behaviours in working independently or as part of a team developing, implementing, maintaining and improving administrative services.

The training is delivered by our sector specialist tutors and supported by a workplace mentor, both sharing their wealth of knowledge and experience. Through regular contact and visits training is supported by virtual Interactive workshops and enriched resources that retain an apprentice's focus and motivation as they progress through their structured programme.

This apprenticeship will positively impact the future of the apprentice, employer's organisation and our wider community.

Provider information

The Childcare Company/ Impact Futures is a national Ofsted Grade 2 provider of fully recognised apprenticeships in Childcare, Team Leading, Business Administration, Customer Service, Management, Adult Health and Social Care using our award-winning online training programme.

Our apprentices are guided and supported by one of our sector expert tutors from the start and throughout their learning journey with the employer following progression using our online system.

By championing lifelong learning that inspires apprentices to continue their own professional development after they achieve, we contribute to providing a skilled workforce we can all be be proud of.
